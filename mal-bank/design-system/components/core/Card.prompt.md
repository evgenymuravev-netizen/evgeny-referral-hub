**Card** — surface container with Mal's soft 20px radius, hairline border, and light diffuse shadow.

```jsx
<Card variant="default" padding="lg">…</Card>
<Card variant="mesh" interactive>Feature</Card>
```

Variants: `default` | `elevated` | `outline` | `mesh` (iridescent fill for feature tiles). `padding`: `none` | `sm` | `md` | `lg`. `interactive` lifts the card 3px on hover.
