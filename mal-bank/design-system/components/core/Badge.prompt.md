**Badge** — small status/label pill. Soft tinted fills by default; solid or outline when needed.

```jsx
<Badge tone="success" dot>Approved</Badge>
<Badge tone="iris" variant="soft">Halal</Badge>
<Badge tone="neutral" variant="outline" size="sm">Pending</Badge>
```

Tones: `neutral` | `iris` | `success` | `warning` | `danger` | `info`. Variants: `soft` (default) | `solid` | `outline`. `dot` prefixes a status dot.
