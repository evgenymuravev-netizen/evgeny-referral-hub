**Toast** — transient notification with a tone accent bar. You control positioning/stacking.

```jsx
<Toast tone="success" title="Transfer sent" message="AED 250 to Layla" icon={<Icon name="check" size={18} />} onClose={dismiss} />
```

Tones: `neutral` | `success` | `warning` | `danger` | `info`. Props: `title`, `message`, `icon`, `onClose`.
